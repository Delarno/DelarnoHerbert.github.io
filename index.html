About Me

Hello! I am a 21 year old Digital Designer and Computer Aided Draught Designer with a large variety of skills under my belt and an inspiration to use them to the best of my ability. I always strive to improve and become efficient in any task I am set to achieve but I am always looking out to learn something new and find new interests.

Hobbies

Motorsports

Cars being a huge passion of mine my whole life has led to me to be invested in car mechanics and general car culture. In my spare time I like to work on my project car; A 1996 Toyota Celica GT (ST202).


Music Production

Listening to music has always been a mainstay while studying long projects and lately I have gained interest in analysing genres such as Underground Hip-hop and the wide range of samples they often using spanning from 70s Brazilian classics to experimental techno songs, as such I started to learn music production as a small hobby. The music within my showcase videos are all demos of what I have made so let me know what you think!
